About Me

Who am I?

Hello friend! I’m Hakan, a developer based in Istanbul, a recent graduate of Boğaziçi University Foreign Language Education Department, and a huge Tron nerd who thinks Disney made a big mistake by not giving the green light for the third movie.

What do I do?

I'm a full-stack web developer who works with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C#, ASP.NET Core, Entity Framework Core, MSSQL, MySQL, and many other modern web technologies. In my professional work, I think programmatically about complex business problems and create web based solutions to those problems. Apart from my current tech stack, I worked with other technologies in the past such as Node.js, React, MongoDB, Python, PHP, VBScript, VBA, and SQLite. I see challenges as learning opportunities, which motivates me to adapt any technology that can better fulfill the requirements of a given project.

Where do I share what I learn & develop?

As a developer who tries to keep up with the current status quo, I see no other practice that's better than writing what I learn. That's why I try to spare time to write tutorials, articles, essays, and other writings about the technologies that I'm currently working with and technologies that I'm learning. You can read my blog to access all my writings.

How can you contact me?

People say that I'm quite approachable, but everything's better when tested. So send an email in my way. Perhaps we can chat about a requirement of your business, a startup idea that you have, a website that you want to build, some prime developer memes that you want to share, or anything!
